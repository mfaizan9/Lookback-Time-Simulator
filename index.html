Star and Observer

The star is at the left and the observer is at the right; the distance between them is given in light-years (ly). The thought bubble shows what the observer sees at the moment marked by the cursor on the timeline. Press go supernova to make the star explode; the expanding circle is the light from the explosion, and the observer only learns of the explosion when that circle reaches them.
